## 1. About ITEN

**ITEN (IT Education Nepal)** is a **free, non-profit, volunteer-driven organization** dedicated to making quality technology education accessible to every individual — regardless of their location, financial background, or prior experience.

We are a passionate community of educators, developers, and volunteers united by a single belief: **everyone deserves access to quality technology education.** Through our YouTube channel, website, and open-source projects, we deliver practical, career-relevant IT knowledge entirely for free.

> "We break down barriers of geography, language, and cost so that no learner is left behind in the digital age."

---

### 1.1 Our Mission

To provide **free, high-quality, and practical technology education** to students, learners, and aspiring IT professionals worldwide — with a special focus on underserved communities across Nepal and South Asia.

- 🎓 Deliver free technology courses and workshops
- 💻 Build open-source learning resources accessible to all
- 🌐 Connect learners with experienced mentors globally
- 🚀 Support community-driven technology initiatives
- 🤝 Eliminate financial and geographic barriers to IT education

---

### 1.2 Our Vision

To create a world where **every learner**, regardless of socioeconomic status or geography, has the opportunity to **learn, grow, and thrive** in the technology industry.

We envision a digitally empowered Nepal and a global community where knowledge is a right, not a privilege.

---

### 1.3 Our Values

| Value | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Accessibility** | Education is free and available to everyone, always |
| **Quality** | We maintain high educational standards in all our content |
| **Community** | We grow together — learners, mentors, and volunteers |
| **Transparency** | We operate openly with no hidden costs or agendas |
| **Inclusion** | We welcome learners of all backgrounds and skill levels |
| **Empowerment** | We equip learners with skills for real-world impact |

## 2. What We Offer

### 2.1 Free Courses & Learning Paths

We provide structured, beginner-to-advanced learning paths across a wide range of technology domains, all completely free:

| Domain | Topics Covered |
|--------|---------------|
| **Web Development** | HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, PHP |
| **Programming** | Python, C, C++, Java, problem solving |
| **Database** | MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB basics |
| **Networking & OS** | Computer fundamentals, Linux, networking basics |
| **Office & Productivity** | MS Office, Google Workspace, typing skills |
| **Career Skills** | Resume writing, interview prep, freelancing basics |

All courses are delivered via our **[YouTube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@iteducationnepal-6725)** in **Nepali language** for maximum accessibility.

---

### 2.2 Mentorship & Guidance

Beyond courses, ITEN provides a human-centered support system:

- **1-on-1 Guidance** — Personalized sessions to address individual learning challenges
- **Career Mentorship** — Roadmaps and advice from industry professionals
- **Motivation Sessions** — Helping students stay consistent and build confidence
- **Doubt Clearing** — Community-driven Q&A via Telegram and social platforms
- **Project Reviews** — Feedback on learners' projects from experienced volunteers

---

### 2.3 Community & Events

ITEN is more than just a learning platform — it's a thriving community:

- **Telegram Community** — Active learners sharing resources and helping each other
- **Live Sessions** — Periodic live classes and Q&A sessions on YouTube
- **Workshops** — Focused, hands-on workshops on in-demand skills
- **Hackathons & Challenges** — Community events to apply and showcase skills
- **Study Groups** — Peer-learning circles for collaborative growth

## 6. Get Involved

ITEN runs entirely on the passion and dedication of volunteers. Here are the ways you can make a difference:

### 6.1 Contribute Code

Help us build better open-source learning tools:
- Fix bugs or improve existing projects
- Build new features for our learning platforms
- Review and improve documentation
- Submit pull requests to any of our public repositories

### 6.2 Volunteer & Mentor

Share your knowledge with the next generation of IT professionals:
- **Become a Mentor** — Guide students on their learning journeys
- **Create Content** — Write tutorials, make videos, or design infographics
- **Conduct Workshops** — Host live sessions on topics you're passionate about
- **Review Submissions** — Help learners improve their projects and assignments

### 6.3 Spread the Word

Help us reach more learners who need us:
- Share our YouTube channel with students and friends
- Recommend ITEN in schools, colleges, and communities
- Follow and engage with us on social media
- Write about ITEN in blogs, forums, or community groups

### 6.4 Support & Donate

Keep our platform free and sustainable:
- Financial contributions help us cover operational costs
- Equipment and resource donations support content creation
- Every contribution — big or small — makes an impact

📩 **Interested in volunteering or partnering?** Email us at [helpdesk@iteducationnepal.org](mailto:helpdesk@iteducationnepal.org)

## 7. How to Contribute

We follow a standard open-source contribution workflow:

```
1. Fork the repository you want to contribute to
2. Create a new branch: git checkout -b feature/your-feature-name
3. Make your changes and commit: git commit -m "Add: description of change"
4. Push to your fork: git push origin feature/your-feature-name
5. Open a Pull Request — describe your changes clearly
```

**Before contributing**, please:
- Read the `CONTRIBUTING.md` in the relevant repository
- Check existing issues and discussions to avoid duplication
- Follow our code style and documentation standards
- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions

**New to open source?** We have beginner-friendly issues labeled `good first issue` — perfect for your first contribution!
